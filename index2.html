AMALGAM INNOVATIONS
INTRODUCTION

Amalgam Innovations came into being when the four friendships were forged with the purpose of web development. All four partners have a passion for wines and beers. These passions range from simply enjoying the multitude and complexity of flavors to actually brewing their own beverages.

Thomas Burchardt has a multitude of experience from being a science teacher, entrepreneurial work, and now web developer. He has consistently been able to provide ideas and overcome obstacles to bring those ideas to fruition.

Karsten Rabe is a computer and technology aficionado. He has worked abroad in the peace corps, is multi-lingual, and is exceptionally creative. During his time as a web developer others have learned to look to him for creative ways to solve web application problems.

Calvin Leung is very attentive to user interface development and approaches all of his tasks with a sense of style that is very admirable. He has excelled at working in a team and bringing the best of Amalgam Innovations forward. His ability to talk through problems and react to changes quickly is a major credit to the company.

Gregory May is an eclectic individual. He has been an engineer, electrician, writer, and now web developer. He enjoys working with big data and providing unique viewpoints to whatever situation arises.
